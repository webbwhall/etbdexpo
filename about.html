What is the ETBD Expo?

Explore the creativity and innovation of ETBD students

The Emerging Technology in Business + Design and Games + Simulation majors blend creativity, design, storytelling, and digital tools to prepare students for careers in digital marketing, content creation, UX/UI design, game development, and immersive media.

The ETBD Expo showcases semester-long student projects across these areas, highlighting both the diversity of the program and the talent of its students.
